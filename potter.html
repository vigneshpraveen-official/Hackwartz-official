Technical Events

PARCHEMENT PRESENTATION

Step into the halls of Hogwarts and present your ideas with knowledge and creativity. The Paper Presentation event provides a platform for students to showcase innovative and research-oriented work before a panel of expert judges.

INVERTIO

Invertio is a coding and web-designing challenge where participants analyze the given output and recreate the required logic or webpage accurately within the allotted time.

HORCRUX QUIZ

The Horcrux Quiz is a fast-paced quiz event designed to test participants' knowledge, logic, and presence of mind. Teams will compete across multiple rounds, with only the best advancing further.

WEB CRUST

Web Crust is a creative web designing event that tests participants' skills in layout design, visual aesthetics, and basic functionality within a limited time.

Non-Technical Events

REVILO

Revelio is an interactive guessing game where participants test their deduction and communication skills. One participant will wear a headband containing an image, while the opposing participant provides single-word or short responses to help identify the image without directly revealing the answer.

EXPECTO BEATS

Expecto Beats is an engaging music-based event where participants test their listening skills by guessing dialogues and identifying missing song lyrics.

IPL AUCTION

IPL Auction is a strategic team-based event where participants act as franchise owners and build their teams through a simulated auction process.
